### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Case, who is struggling to cope with the loss of his best friend, Walker. He finds solace in a list Walker left behind, which he is determined to complete. Meanwhile, Winnie, a young woman from a troubled family, is trying to support her siblings while pursuing her passion for rodeo. Their paths cross when Case ropes Winnie into competing for the ranch, and they begin to develop feelings for each other. The narrative is engaging, and the characters' growth is well-crafted.
