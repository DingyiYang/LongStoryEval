### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows six high school students who witness a horrific crime in an abandoned coal mine, leading them to form a pact of silence. Twenty years later, they are reunited to uncover the truth behind Maddy's murder. The plot is engaging, and the twist of the group's reunion adds depth to the narrative.
