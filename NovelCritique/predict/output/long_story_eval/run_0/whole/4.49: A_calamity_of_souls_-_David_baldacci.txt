### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The novel's pacing is excellent, keeping me engaged from start to finish. The plot is both gripping and thought-provoking, featuring unexpected twists that kept me guessing until the very end.

**Characters:**
The characters are well-developed and relatable, with Jack Lee's transformation from a passive bystander to an active advocate for justice being particularly compelling. The dynamic between Jack and Desiree DuBose is both engaging and realistic, as they navigate their differences while working together to achieve a common goal.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing is both engaging and accessible, making the story easy to follow and enjoy. The vivid descriptions of the setting and characters enhance the overall experience.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The novel is set in 1968, a time of great turmoil and change. The author skillfully captures the racial tensions and societal upheaval of the era, creating a vivid and immersive atmosphere.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of racial injustice, systemic oppression, and the struggle for equality. It highlights the challenges faced by Black individuals and the importance of white allies in the fight for justice.

**Emotional Impact:**
The novel is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of the racial injustices faced by Black individuals. The author's writing evokes a strong sense of empathy and outrage, making the story both impactful and thought-provoking.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed this novel and found it to be a compelling read. The author's writing style and the story's themes kept me engaged from start to finish.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
I was drawn to this novel because of its historical setting and the promise of a gripping mystery. The author's previous work, *The Last House Guest*, had left a lasting impression on me, and I was eager to see how they would tackle this new project.

### Overall Assessment:
This novel is a gripping and thought-provoking exploration of racial injustice and systemic oppression. With its well-developed characters, engaging plot, and vivid setting, it is a compelling read that leaves a lasting impression. The author's writing style and ability to evoke strong emotions make this a standout work.

### Score: 4.5
