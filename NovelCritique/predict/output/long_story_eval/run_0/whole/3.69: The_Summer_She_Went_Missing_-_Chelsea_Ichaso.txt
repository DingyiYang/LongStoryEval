### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Paige as she uncovers the truth behind her friend Audrey's disappearance, which is intricately linked to the town's dark secrets. The plot is engaging, and the mystery surrounding Audrey's disappearance kept me hooked from start to finish.

**Characters:**
Paige is a determined and brave protagonist who drives the investigation into Audrey's disappearance. Her friendship with Dylan adds depth to the story, and their collaboration is essential in uncovering the truth. Audrey's character is well-developed, and her journey from victim to survivor is both compelling and emotionally resonant.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing is engaging and well-paced, making the story easy to follow and keeping me invested in the mystery.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The setting of Clearwater Ridge is vividly described, immersing me in the town's atmosphere and adding depth to the story.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of friendship, resilience, and the struggle against privilege and injustice. It highlights the importance of standing up for oneself and others, even in the face of adversity.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally charged, particularly in its portrayal of Audrey's trauma and her journey towards healing. The themes of resilience and friendship are deeply moving.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed this book and found it hard to put down. The mystery and emotional depth kept me engaged from start to finish.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
This book is a must-read for fans of mystery and thriller genres, particularly those who enjoy stories about friendship and resilience.

### Overall Assessment:
This novel is a gripping mystery with a well-developed plot, relatable characters, and a vividly described setting. It explores themes of friendship, resilience, and standing up against injustice, making it a deeply emotionally resonant read. I highly recommend it to fans of the genre.

### Score: 4.5
