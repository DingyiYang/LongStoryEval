### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Sylvia, Junietta, and Adelaide as they navigate the aftermath of Arthur Stanhope III's death, grappling with the revelation that his will leaves his company and fortune to the nearest male heir. The narrative centers on Sylvia's efforts to secure a wealthy husband for Adelaide, while Junietta pushes for her granddaughter to forge her own path. The plot is engaging, and the family's journey to confront painful truths and reshape their legacy is both compelling and emotionally resonant.

**Characters:**
Sylvia, Junietta, and Adelaide are well-developed characters, each navigating their own struggles and growth. Sylvia's transformation from a society wife to a woman seeking personal fulfillment is particularly compelling. The supporting characters, including Howard Forsythe and Andrew Browne, add depth to the narrative, enriching the family's dynamics and emotional arcs.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing is engaging and immersive, making it easy to become fully invested in the characters and their struggles. The historical setting is vividly portrayed, enhancing the overall experience.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The novel is set in New York during the Gilded Age, providing a rich and immersive backdrop for the story. The author skillfully captures the era's opulence and societal expectations, making the setting feel authentic and engaging.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of family, legacy, and personal growth, particularly through the lens of women's roles in society. It highlights the challenges women faced during the Gilded Age and the importance of embracing change and self-discovery.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of Sylvia's journey and the family's struggles. The themes of love, integrity, and resilience are deeply felt, making the narrative both moving and impactful.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed this novel and found it hard to put down. The engaging plot, well-developed characters, and immersive setting made it a memorable read.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
As a fan of historical fiction, I was thrilled to discover this novel. The Gilded Age setting and the exploration of women's roles during that time were particularly appealing to me.

### Overall Assessment:
This novel is a compelling and emotionally resonant historical fiction that explores themes of family, legacy, and personal growth. With well-developed characters, an immersive setting, and a gripping plot, it is a memorable read that I highly recommend.

### Score: 4.5
