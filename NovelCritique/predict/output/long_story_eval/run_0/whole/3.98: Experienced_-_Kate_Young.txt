### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's pacing was uneven, with some sections feeling rushed and others dragging on. The plot lacked depth, making it difficult to fully engage with the narrative.

**Characters:**
The characters lacked depth and development, making it challenging to connect with them on a personal level.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing style was inconsistent, with some sections feeling overly descriptive and others lacking sufficient detail.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The setting was underdeveloped, failing to provide a rich or immersive backdrop for the story.

**Themes:**
The exploration of queer identity and relationships was handled poorly, feeling forced and lacking authenticity.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story failed to evoke any significant emotions, leaving me feeling disconnected and uninvested.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I found the book to be a chore to read, and I struggled to stay engaged throughout.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
I was initially excited to read this book, but it ultimately failed to meet my expectations.

### Overall Assessment:
Overall, this book was a disappointment. The uneven pacing, lack of character development, and poor handling of queer themes made it a chore to read. The writing style was inconsistent, and the story failed to evoke any emotions. I would not recommend this book to anyone.

### Score: 1.5
