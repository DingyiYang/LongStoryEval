### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's pacing was uneven, with some sections feeling rushed and others dragging on. The plot was engaging, but the lack of a clear resolution left me wanting more.

**Characters:**
The characters were well-developed and engaging, but their relationships felt underexplored. I would have appreciated more insight into their dynamics and interactions.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing was engaging, and the author's use of language was impressive. However, the pacing issues and lack of resolution detracted from the overall experience.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The world-building was impressive, with a richly detailed setting that added depth to the story.

**Themes:**
The exploration of colonialism and capitalism was thought-provoking and well-executed.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story had moments of emotional depth, but the lack of resolution left me feeling unsatisfied.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed the story and found it engaging, but the pacing issues and lack of resolution left me wanting more.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
I was excited to read this book, and it met many of my expectations. However, the pacing issues and lack of resolution left me feeling somewhat disappointed.

### Overall Assessment:
Overall, I enjoyed the story and found it engaging, but the pacing issues and lack of resolution left me wanting more. The world-building, characters, and exploration of colonialism and capitalism were highlights, but the story could have benefited from a more balanced pacing and a clearer resolution.

### Score: 3.5
