### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Xishi, a young woman who uses her beauty to infiltrate the Wu court as a spy, seduce the king, and weaken the enemy from within. The plot is engaging, and the historical fantasy setting adds depth to the narrative.

**Characters:**
Xishi is a complex and well-developed protagonist, driven by her desire for vengeance and her willingness to sacrifice her own happiness for the sake of her people. The romance between Xishi and Fanli is beautifully crafted, adding emotional depth to the story.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing is exquisite, with vivid descriptions that bring the characters and setting to life. The author's use of metaphors and imagery is particularly noteworthy, enhancing the overall reading experience.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The historical fantasy setting is richly detailed, immersing readers in the world of ancient China. The author's use of historical figures and events adds authenticity to the narrative.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of womanhood, war, sacrifice, and love, all of which are deeply intertwined. The author's portrayal of Xishi's journey is both poignant and thought-provoking.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of Xishi's sacrifices and the impact of war on civilians. The romance between Xishi and Fanli adds a touching layer to the narrative.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The novel is a captivating read, with a plot that keeps readers engaged from start to finish. The emotional depth and historical fantasy elements make it a standout in the genre.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The novel exceeded my expectations, delivering a rich and emotionally resonant story that I will not soon forget.

### Overall Assessment:
A Song to Drown Rivers is a beautifully written historical fantasy novel that combines a compelling plot, rich world-building, and emotionally resonant characters. The author's use of metaphors and imagery, along with the exploration of themes like womanhood and sacrifice, makes this a standout read. I highly recommend it to fans of historical fantasy and romance.

### Score: 4.5
