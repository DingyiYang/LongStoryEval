### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows three young women who embark on a tech-free vacation at a resort in Majorca, where they encounter unexpected challenges and personal growth. The narrative is engaging, and the plot effectively explores themes of self-discovery and friendship.

**Characters:**
The three main characters—Luna, Rory, and Jodie—are well-developed and relatable, each navigating their own struggles and triumphs. Their journeys of self-discovery and growth are compelling and resonate deeply.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing is engaging and accessible, making the story easy to follow and enjoy.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The resort in Majorca serves as a picturesque backdrop for the characters' journeys, enhancing the story's charm and setting.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of self-discovery, friendship, and personal growth, making it a heartwarming and relatable read.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, offering a sense of hope and renewal that is both uplifting and inspiring.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The story is a delightful and engaging read, perfect for anyone seeking a lighthearted yet meaningful tale.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
This novel is ideal for readers seeking a heartwarming and uplifting story about friendship and self-discovery.

### Overall Assessment:
This novel is a delightful and heartwarming story about friendship and self-discovery. With its engaging plot, relatable characters, and uplifting themes, it's a perfect read for anyone looking for a lighthearted yet meaningful tale.

### Score: 4.0
