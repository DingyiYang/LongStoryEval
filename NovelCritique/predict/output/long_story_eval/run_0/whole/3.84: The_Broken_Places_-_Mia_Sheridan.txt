### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's pacing was excellent, keeping me engaged from start to finish. The plot was well-developed and featured a compelling mystery that kept me guessing until the end.

**Characters:**
Lennon Gray and Ambrose DeMarce are well-developed characters with rich backstories that enhance their depth. Their relationship is both sweet and intense, adding significant emotional weight to the story.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing is engaging and skillfully crafted, making the story both enjoyable and immersive.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The author's vivid descriptions of San Francisco and the homeless community create a rich and immersive setting that enhances the story's realism.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of trauma, healing, and the complexities of human relationships, all of which are handled with sensitivity and depth.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally intense, with moments of tenderness and heartbreak that left a lasting impression.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed this novel and found it to be a compelling read.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The novel's blend of romance, mystery, and suspense was executed flawlessly, making it a standout read.

### Overall Assessment:
This novel is a masterfully crafted blend of romance, mystery, and suspense, with well-developed characters, a gripping plot, and a richly described setting. The emotional depth and intensity of the story make it a memorable read.

### Score: 4.5
