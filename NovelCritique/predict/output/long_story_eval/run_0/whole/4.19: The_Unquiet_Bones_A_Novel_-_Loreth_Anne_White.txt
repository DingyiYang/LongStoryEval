### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Jane Munro, a pregnant homicide detective, as she investigates the discovery of human bones beneath an old chapel. The plot is engaging, and the mystery surrounding the bones and the Shoreview Six group keeps me hooked.

**Characters:**
Jane Munro is a well-developed character, and her pregnancy adds a unique layer to her investigation. The Shoreview Six group is intriguing, and their secrets are skillfully revealed throughout the story.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing is engaging and well-paced, making it easy to follow the story and its twists.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The setting of the old chapel and the surrounding woods adds a haunting and atmospheric backdrop to the story.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of family, trauma, and the consequences of past actions, which are central to the mystery.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of Jane's pregnancy and her connection to the case.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The novel is a quick and engaging read, making it perfect for a weekend or a short vacation.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The novel is a gripping mystery that delivers on its promise of a thrilling investigation.

### Overall Assessment:
The Unquiet Bones is a gripping mystery with a well-developed protagonist, an intriguing plot, and a haunting setting. The novel delivers on its promise of a thrilling investigation, making it a perfect read for fans of the genre.

### Score: 4.0
