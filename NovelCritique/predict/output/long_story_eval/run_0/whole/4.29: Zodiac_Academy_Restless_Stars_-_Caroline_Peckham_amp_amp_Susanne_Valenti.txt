### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story concludes with a satisfying resolution, providing a sense of closure and fulfillment. The final battle is intense and well-executed, leaving a lasting impression.

**Characters:**
The characters' growth and development throughout the story are commendable. Their journeys, especially the sisters' transformation from rebellious teenagers to powerful queens, are both compelling and well-executed.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing style is engaging and immersive, making it easy to become fully invested in the story.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The magical world of Solaria is richly detailed and immersive, with a compelling blend of magic and politics that keeps me engaged.

**Themes:**
The story's exploration of power, rebellion, and the complexities of leadership is both thought-provoking and well-executed.

**Emotional Impact:**
The emotional depth of the story is exceptional, with moments of joy, sadness, and triumph that resonate deeply.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed this book and found it hard to put down. The story's pacing kept me engaged from start to finish.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The book's dark fantasy romance genre is executed perfectly, delivering a thrilling and emotionally charged experience.

### Overall Assessment:
This book is a fantastic conclusion to the series, delivering a satisfying and emotionally charged story. The characters' growth, the rich world-building, and the intense plot make it a must-read for fans of dark fantasy romance.

### Score: 4.5
