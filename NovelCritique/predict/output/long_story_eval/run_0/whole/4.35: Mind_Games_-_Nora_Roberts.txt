### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Thea Fox, a young girl who possesses psychic abilities, as she navigates her life in Redbud Hollow, Kentucky. The narrative is engaging, and the plot is well-paced, keeping me invested in Thea's journey.

**Characters:**
Thea Fox is a compelling protagonist, and her journey of self-discovery and empowerment is both inspiring and relatable. The supporting characters, including her grandmother, brother, and love interest, add depth and warmth to the story.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing is engaging and accessible, making the story easy to follow and enjoy.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The setting of Redbud Hollow, Kentucky, is vividly described, immersing me in the story's atmosphere.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of trauma, resilience, and the power of love and family. Thea's journey of overcoming her past and embracing her gifts is both inspiring and emotionally resonant.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally rich, with moments of joy, sadness, and triumph that left me feeling deeply connected to Thea's journey.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed this novel and found it hard to put down. The engaging plot, relatable characters, and emotional depth made it a memorable read.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The novel's blend of romance, suspense, and supernatural elements creates a unique and captivating narrative.

### Overall Assessment:
Mind Games is a captivating and emotionally rich novel that combines romance, suspense, and supernatural elements with a strong focus on trauma, resilience, and family. The author's writing is engaging, and the story is both inspiring and memorable.

### Score: 4.5
