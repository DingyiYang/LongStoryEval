### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Sori, a K-pop idol who must navigate her career and personal life while dealing with her mother's expectations and her father's presidential ambitions. The plot is engaging, but the pacing occasionally feels rushed, particularly in the latter half of the book.

**Characters:**
Sori's journey is compelling, and her growth throughout the story is well-developed. However, the supporting characters, such as her mother and father, feel underutilized, leaving their motivations and actions somewhat unclear.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing is clear and engaging, making it easy to follow the story. However, the pacing issues in the latter half of the book detract from the overall experience.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The K-pop industry setting is well-executed, providing a unique and immersive backdrop for the story.

**Themes:**
The book explores themes of identity, ambition, and the challenges of balancing personal and professional life. These themes are well-developed and resonate with readers.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally engaging, particularly in its portrayal of Sori's struggles and growth. However, the rushed pacing in the latter half of the book diminishes the emotional impact.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The book is an enjoyable read, with a unique setting and compelling themes. However, the pacing issues and underdeveloped supporting characters prevent it from being a standout.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The book delivers on its promise of a K-pop romance, but the rushed pacing and underdeveloped supporting characters leave some aspects of the story feeling incomplete.

### Overall Assessment:
Overall, *ASAP* is a solid debut novel with a unique setting and compelling themes. While the story is enjoyable and emotionally engaging, the pacing issues and underdeveloped supporting characters prevent it from being a perfect read. Fans of K-pop and romance will likely appreciate the book, but readers seeking a more polished narrative may find it lacking.

### Score: 3.5
