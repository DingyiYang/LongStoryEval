### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's pacing was excellent, keeping me engaged from start to finish. The plot was well-developed, featuring a mix of adventure, romance, and suspense that kept me hooked.

**Characters:**
Dahlia and Winston's chemistry was electric, and their banter was both witty and engaging. Their relationship evolved naturally, and I appreciated how they supported each other throughout the journey.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing style was engaging and easy to follow, making the story a quick and enjoyable read.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The Colorado Rockies provided a stunning backdrop for the story, and the author's vivid descriptions made me feel as though I was right there with Dahlia and Winston.

**Themes:**
The story beautifully explores themes of resilience, trust, and the power of relationships. Dahlia and Winston's journey is both inspiring and heartwarming.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story elicited a range of emotions, from excitement and suspense to warmth and joy. The emotional depth of the characters and their relationships made the experience truly memorable.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed this book and found it hard to put down. The perfect blend of adventure, romance, and suspense kept me engaged from start to finish.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
This book exceeded my expectations in every way. It was a delightful surprise that I'm so glad I took a chance on.

### Overall Assessment:
This book was a delightful surprise, offering a perfect blend of adventure, romance, and suspense set against the stunning backdrop of the Colorado Rockies. The characters were well-developed, their chemistry was electric, and the story left me feeling inspired and emotionally satisfied. I highly recommend it to anyone looking for a fun and engaging read.

### Score: 4.5
