### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's pacing was uneven, with some sections feeling rushed and others dragging on. The plot was engaging, but the lack of a clear resolution left me feeling unsatisfied.

**Characters:**
The characters were well-developed, and their relationships added depth to the story. However, the absence of a clear resolution left me feeling uncertain about their futures.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing was engaging, and the story's magical realism was executed beautifully.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The magical realism was a standout feature, and the world-building was both imaginative and immersive.

**Themes:**
The story's exploration of the power of books and the importance of protecting them was both meaningful and well-executed.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story's emotional depth was a highlight, particularly in the moments between Cassie and her grandfather.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed the story and found it to be a delightful read.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
I was initially hesitant due to the mixed reviews, but I'm glad I gave it a chance.

### Overall Assessment:
Overall, I thoroughly enjoyed this magical realism novel, despite its uneven pacing and lack of a clear resolution. The characters, world-building, and emotional depth made it a memorable read, and I'm excited to see where the story goes next.

### Score: 4.0
