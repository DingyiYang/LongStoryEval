### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's pacing was uneven, with some sections feeling rushed and others dragging on. The plot twist involving the Ministry's true intentions was predictable, and the resolution felt rushed and unsatisfying.

**Characters:**
The characters were well-developed, and their relationships were engaging. However, the romantic subplot felt forced and overshadowed the main plot, which was disappointing.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing was engaging, and the author's use of language was impressive. However, the pacing issues and predictable plot twist detracted from the overall experience.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The world-building was impressive, with a unique and well-executed concept. However, the execution fell short due to pacing issues and a predictable plot twist.

**Themes:**
The story's exploration of time travel and its implications was intriguing, but the execution fell short due to pacing issues and a predictable plot twist.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story had moments of emotional depth, particularly in the relationships between the characters. However, the rushed resolution and predictable plot twist diminished the overall impact.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I was initially excited to read this book, but the pacing issues and predictable plot twist left me feeling disappointed. Despite its potential, the story fell short of my expectations.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
I was excited to read this book, but it didn't meet my expectations. The premise was intriguing, but the execution fell short.

### Overall Assessment:
While the book had a promising premise and well-developed characters, the pacing issues, predictable plot twist, and rushed resolution left me feeling disappointed. Despite its potential, the story didn't meet my expectations, and I wouldn't recommend it to others.

### Score: 2.5
