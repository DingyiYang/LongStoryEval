### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's dual timeline structure was well-executed, allowing for a seamless transition between Shelley's past and present. The mystery surrounding her husband's involvement in her accident kept me engaged and curious about the truth.

**Characters:**
Shelley's journey from a victim of abuse to a survivor and advocate for women's rights was both inspiring and empowering. Her growth and resilience made her a compelling protagonist. The supporting characters, including Matt and Dee, added depth to the narrative, and their relationships with Shelley enriched the story.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing style was engaging and easy to follow, making the story enjoyable from start to finish.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The setting of the women's shelter and the pub added a unique and meaningful backdrop to Shelley's journey.

**Themes:**
The novel powerfully explores themes of domestic violence, resilience, and the importance of support systems. Shelley's journey serves as a testament to the strength of women and the impact of supportive relationships.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story elicited a range of emotions, from sadness and frustration to hope and inspiration. Shelley's struggles and triumphs resonated deeply, making her journey both relatable and impactful.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed this book and found it hard to put down. The engaging plot and well-developed characters kept me invested throughout.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The book's premise was intriguing, and the author's ability to balance mystery and emotional depth made it a compelling read.

### Overall Assessment:
This novel is a powerful and inspiring story about resilience, hope, and the strength of women. Shelley's journey from victim to survivor is both heartbreaking and uplifting, and the supporting characters add depth to the narrative. The dual timeline structure and mystery surrounding her husband's involvement kept me engaged, and the themes of domestic violence and support systems resonated deeply. I highly recommend this book to anyone looking for a story that inspires and moves them.

### Score: 4.5
