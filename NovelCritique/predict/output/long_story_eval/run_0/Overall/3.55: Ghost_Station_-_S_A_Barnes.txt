### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's pacing was inconsistent, with some sections feeling rushed and others dragging on. The plot twist involving the alien technology was predictable and lacked surprise.

**Characters:**
The crew's dynamics were well-developed, and their interactions added depth to the story. However, the protagonist's internal struggles felt underdeveloped, making it difficult to connect with her on a deeper level.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing style was engaging, and the author's use of language effectively conveyed the characters' emotions and the eerie atmosphere of the abandoned planet.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The abandoned planet and the crew's mission to uncover its secrets were intriguing elements that added depth to the story.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of isolation, mental health, and the consequences of unchecked power, which are thought-provoking and relevant.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story's emotional impact was diminished by the lack of depth in the protagonist's character development.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The novel was an enjoyable read, but its flaws prevented it from being truly memorable.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The novel's premise was intriguing, and the concept of a crew exploring an abandoned planet was captivating.

### Overall Assessment:
While the novel had an intriguing premise and explored thought-provoking themes, its inconsistent pacing, predictable plot twist, and underdeveloped protagonist made it a mixed experience. Despite these flaws, the story was still enjoyable and left me curious about the author's future work.

### Score: 3.2
