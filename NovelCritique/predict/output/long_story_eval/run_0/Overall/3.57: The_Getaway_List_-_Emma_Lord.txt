### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Riley as she embarks on a journey of self-discovery in New York City, reuniting with her childhood friend Tom and exploring the city with her new friends. The plot is engaging, and the characters' growth is well-developed.

**Characters:**
Riley's journey of self-discovery is compelling, and her relationships with Tom and her new friends are well-developed. The supporting characters, including Mariella and Luca, add depth to the story.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing is engaging and easy to follow, making the story enjoyable from start to finish.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The vibrant backdrop of New York City enhances the story's charm and adds to its overall appeal.

**Themes:**
The story explores themes of self-discovery, friendship, and the importance of embracing change. Riley's journey of finding herself and her place in the world is both relatable and inspiring.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of Riley's struggles and triumphs. The characters' growth and relationships are deeply felt.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The story is a delightful and engaging read, perfect for anyone looking for a lighthearted yet meaningful tale.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
This book is ideal for fans of young adult contemporary romance and self-discovery stories.

### Overall Assessment:
This is a delightful and engaging young adult contemporary romance that explores themes of self-discovery, friendship, and growth. With its relatable protagonist, well-developed relationships, and vibrant setting, it's a story that will leave readers feeling inspired and uplifted.

### Score: 4.0
