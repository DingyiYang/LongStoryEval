### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Case, who is struggling to cope with the loss of his best friend, Walker. He finds solace in a list Walker left behind, which he is determined to complete. Meanwhile, Winnie, a young woman from a troubled family, is trying to support her siblings while pursuing her passion for rodeo. Their paths cross when Case ropes Winnie into competing for the ranch, and they begin to develop feelings for each other. The narrative is engaging, and the characters' growth is well-crafted.

**Characters:**
Case and Winnie are well-developed characters who navigate their grief and personal struggles with authenticity. Their relationship is sweet and heartwarming, with a strong focus on mutual support and understanding.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing is engaging and easy to follow, making the story enjoyable from start to finish.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The rodeo setting is vividly described, immersing readers in the world of competition and the characters' passions.

**Themes:**
The story explores themes of grief, resilience, and the importance of pursuing one's dreams. It highlights the challenges faced by Winnie and Case, as well as their determination to overcome them.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of grief and the characters' struggles. The heartwarming moments between Case and Winnie add depth and warmth to the narrative.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The story is engaging and easy to follow, making it a quick and enjoyable read.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The story is a heartwarming romance that delivers on its promise of a sweet and emotional journey.

### Overall Assessment:
Even If It Breaks Your Heart is a heartwarming and emotionally resonant romance that explores themes of grief, resilience, and following one's dreams. With well-developed characters, a vivid setting, and a sweet love story, this novel is a delightful read.

### Score: 4.5
