### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's pacing was uneven, with some sections feeling rushed and others dragging on. The plot was engaging, but the lack of a clear resolution left me feeling unsatisfied.

**Characters:**
Pell's character development was impressive, and her growth throughout the story was well-executed. However, the supporting characters lacked depth, making it difficult to connect with them.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing was engaging, and the author's use of descriptive language effectively brought the world to life.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The world-building was impressive, with a richly detailed setting that felt immersive and well-developed.

**Themes:**
The story's exploration of the relationship between humans and technology was thought-provoking and added depth to the narrative.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story's emotional impact was significant, particularly in the final chapters, which left a lasting impression.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The story was engaging, and I found myself invested in Pell's journey.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The book's premise was intriguing, and I was excited to dive into the story.

### Overall Assessment:
Overall, I enjoyed the book's premise, world-building, and emotional impact. However, the uneven pacing, lack of resolution, and underdeveloped supporting characters left me wanting more. Despite these flaws, the story was engaging, and I would recommend it to fans of science fiction and fantasy.

### Score: 3.5
