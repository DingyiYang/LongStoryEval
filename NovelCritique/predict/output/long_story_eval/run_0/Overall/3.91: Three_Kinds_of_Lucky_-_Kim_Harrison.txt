### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's pacing was inconsistent, with some sections feeling rushed and others dragging on. The plot was engaging, but the lack of a clear resolution left me feeling unsatisfied.

**Characters:**
Petra's journey from a sweeper to a shadow weaver was compelling, but her character development felt incomplete. The supporting cast, including Benedict, Pluck, and Lev, was well-developed and added depth to the narrative.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing style was engaging, and the story's premise was intriguing. However, the inconsistent pacing and lack of resolution detracted from the overall experience.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The world-building was impressive, with a richly detailed magical system and a unique setting that added depth to the story.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of self-discovery, empowerment, and the consequences of one's actions. Petra's journey to embrace her abilities and confront her past is both inspiring and thought-provoking.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story elicited strong emotions, particularly Petra's struggles with her identity and her desire to break free from her predetermined path. However, the lack of a satisfying resolution left me feeling frustrated.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I was initially excited to read this book, but the inconsistent pacing and lack of resolution left me feeling disappointed. Despite its flaws, I still enjoyed the story and would consider reading the next installment.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
I was drawn to this book by its unique premise and the author's previous work. While it didn't fully meet my expectations, it was still an enjoyable read.

### Overall Assessment:
Overall, I enjoyed the story and found the premise and world-building to be strong points. However, the inconsistent pacing, lack of resolution, and incomplete character development left me feeling frustrated. Despite these flaws, I would consider reading the next installment to see how the story unfolds.

### Score: 3.5
