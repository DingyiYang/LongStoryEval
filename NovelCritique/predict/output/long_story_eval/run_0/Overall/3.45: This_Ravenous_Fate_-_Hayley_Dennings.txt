### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's pacing was uneven, with some sections feeling rushed and others dragging on. The plot was engaging, but the lack of a clear resolution left me wanting more.

**Characters:**
The characters were well-developed, and their relationships were compelling. However, the absence of a clear resolution left me feeling unsatisfied.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing was engaging, and the author's use of language was impressive. However, the pacing issues detracted from the overall experience.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The 1920s setting was vividly portrayed, and the reaper mythology was intriguing. However, the world-building felt incomplete, leaving me with unanswered questions.

**Themes:**
The story effectively explored themes of trauma, identity, and the struggle for power. However, the lack of a clear resolution left me feeling unsatisfied.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story was emotionally engaging, and the characters' relationships were deeply felt. However, the unresolved plot left me feeling frustrated.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed the story and was eager to continue reading. However, the pacing issues and lack of resolution left me feeling frustrated.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
I was excited to read this book, and it met many of my expectations. However, the pacing issues and lack of resolution left me feeling disappointed.

### Overall Assessment:
Overall, I enjoyed the story and found the characters and relationships compelling. However, the pacing issues, lack of resolution, and incomplete world-building left me feeling frustrated and unsatisfied. Despite these flaws, I would still recommend the book to others, as it has the potential to be a great read with further development.

### Score: 3.5
