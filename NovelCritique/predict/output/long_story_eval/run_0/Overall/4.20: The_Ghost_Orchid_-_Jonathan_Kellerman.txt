### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows a pool boy who discovers two bodies in a Bel Air property, leading to a complex investigation involving a double murder. The plot is engaging, and the mystery surrounding the victims' identities and the motives behind the crime kept me hooked.

**Characters:**
The characters, particularly the victims, are well-developed and intriguing. Their backstories and motivations add depth to the story, making it more engaging.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing style is engaging and easy to follow, making the story enjoyable from start to finish.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The setting of Bel Air and the wealthy neighborhood adds a unique and intriguing backdrop to the story.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of identity, family dynamics, and the complexities of human relationships, which are thoughtfully developed throughout the narrative.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story evokes a range of emotions, from intrigue to empathy, as the characters' struggles and motivations are revealed.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The novel is a quick and enjoyable read, with a plot that keeps me engaged from start to finish.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The novel delivers on its promise of a thrilling mystery, with a well-crafted plot and engaging characters.

### Overall Assessment:
Overall, this novel is a compelling and well-crafted mystery that combines a gripping plot, intriguing characters, and thought-provoking themes. It's a quick and enjoyable read that leaves a lasting impression.

### Score: 4.5
