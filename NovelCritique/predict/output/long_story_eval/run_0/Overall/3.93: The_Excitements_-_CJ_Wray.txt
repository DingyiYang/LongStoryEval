### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's pacing was uneven, with some sections feeling rushed and others dragging on. The heist plotline, in particular, lacked tension and failed to deliver the expected excitement.

**Characters:**
The characters were well-developed, and their relationships were engaging. However, the heist plotline felt underdeveloped, lacking the necessary tension and excitement.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing was engaging, and the story was well-paced, making it an enjoyable read.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The historical setting was well-executed, and the inclusion of real-life events added depth to the narrative.

**Themes:**
The novel's exploration of family dynamics and the impact of war on individuals and relationships was both poignant and thought-provoking.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story's emotional depth was a standout, particularly in its portrayal of family dynamics and the lasting effects of war.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The novel was an enjoyable read, with a well-crafted narrative that kept me engaged.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The novel's premise was intriguing, and the inclusion of real-life events added a layer of authenticity.

### Overall Assessment:
Overall, the novel was an enjoyable read with strong character development and emotional depth. However, the uneven pacing and underdeveloped heist plotline detracted from the experience. Despite these flaws, the story's exploration of family dynamics and historical context made it a worthwhile read.

### Score: 3.5
