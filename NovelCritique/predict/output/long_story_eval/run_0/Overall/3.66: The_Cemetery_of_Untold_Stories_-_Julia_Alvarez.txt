### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The novel's structure is both unique and captivating, featuring a cemetery where characters from abandoned stories come to life. This concept is both intriguing and well-executed.

**Characters:**
The characters are well-developed and engaging, each with their own distinct voice and story. The author's ability to bring them to life is impressive.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing is both poetic and evocative, capturing the essence of the characters and their stories beautifully.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The cemetery setting is vividly described, creating a unique and immersive atmosphere.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of storytelling, memory, and the power of narratives to shape our understanding of ourselves and others.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of the characters' struggles and triumphs.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The novel is a captivating read, with its unique concept and well-crafted characters keeping me engaged from start to finish.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The novel's premise is both intriguing and well-executed, making it a standout read.

### Overall Assessment:
The Cemetery of Untold Stories is a beautifully written and emotionally resonant novel that explores the power of storytelling and memory. With its unique premise, well-developed characters, and poetic writing, it is a standout read that I highly recommend.

### Score: 4.5
