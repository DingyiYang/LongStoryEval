### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows two women, Emilia Bassano and Melina Green, as they navigate the challenges of being female playwrights in their respective eras. Emilia, a ward of English aristocrats, dreams of writing her own plays, while Melina, a contemporary playwright, faces sexism in the modern theater world. The narrative is presented in intertwining timelines, allowing readers to witness the struggles of both women as they strive to create something beautiful despite the obstacles they encounter.

**Characters:**
Emilia and Melina are both determined and resilient women who refuse to let societal norms define their aspirations. Their journeys are inspiring, and their struggles to overcome the challenges of being female playwrights in their respective eras are deeply relatable.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing is engaging and well-researched, providing a rich historical context that enhances the narrative. The intertwining timelines allow for a seamless exploration of both eras, making the story feel both contemporary and timeless.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The historical setting of 1581 England and the contemporary theater world in Washington Heights are vividly portrayed, immersing readers in the lives of Emilia and Melina. The author's meticulous research ensures that the historical details are accurate and engaging.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of ambition, courage, and desire, highlighting the challenges women face in pursuing their dreams. It also delves into the importance of preserving one's legacy and the impact of societal norms on personal aspirations.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of the struggles faced by women in both eras. The author's ability to connect the past with the present makes the narrative feel both timeless and relevant.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The novel is a captivating read, blending historical fiction with contemporary themes. The intertwining timelines and well-developed characters make it a story that is both engaging and memorable.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The novel's premise is intriguing, blending historical fiction with contemporary themes. The intertwining timelines and the focus on women's struggles in both eras make it a compelling read.

### Overall Assessment:
By Any Other Name is a captivating historical fiction novel that seamlessly blends the past with the present. Through the intertwining timelines of Emilia Bassano and Melina Green, the author explores themes of ambition, courage, and desire, highlighting the challenges women face in pursuing their dreams. The novel is well-researched, emotionally resonant, and engaging, making it a memorable read.

### Score: 4.5
