### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Mel, a bartender who is initially hesitant to open herself to love after a divorce. However, her life changes when she meets Bebe and Kade, a charmingly quirky couple who introduce her to the world of polyamory. The narrative is engaging and well-paced, making it easy to follow and enjoy.

**Characters:**
Mel is a relatable protagonist who undergoes significant growth throughout the story. Her journey from skepticism to embracing love is both heartwarming and inspiring. Bebe and Kade are equally well-developed, adding depth and charm to the narrative. Their dynamic with Mel is authentic and engaging, making their relationships feel genuine and meaningful.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing is engaging and accessible, making the story easy to follow and enjoy. The dialogue is natural and well-executed, adding depth to the characters and their relationships.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The exclusive world of high-end cocktail bars is vividly portrayed, immersing readers in the setting. The author's attention to detail creates a believable and engaging atmosphere.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of love, polyamory, and self-discovery, all of which are handled with sensitivity and authenticity. The author's portrayal of polyamory is particularly noteworthy, as it is depicted in a way that feels both realistic and empowering.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of Mel's journey. Her growth and the relationships she forms are deeply satisfying, leaving a lasting impression on the reader.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The novel is a delightful and engaging read, perfect for fans of romance and polyamory. The author's writing style and the story's pacing make it a quick and enjoyable experience.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The novel delivers on its promise of a polyamorous romance, offering a fresh and engaging take on the genre. The author's portrayal of polyamory is both realistic and empowering, making the story feel authentic and meaningful.

### Overall Assessment:
*Triple Sec* is a delightful and engaging polyamorous romance that explores themes of love, self-discovery, and relationships. With well-developed characters, a vivid setting, and a natural writing style, the novel is a must-read for fans of the genre. The author's portrayal of polyamory is particularly noteworthy, making the story feel authentic and empowering.

### Score: 4.5
