### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The plot was engaging, but the pacing felt uneven, with some sections dragging on while others moved too quickly. The story's conclusion was particularly rushed, leaving me wanting more.

**Characters:**
The characters were well-developed and intriguing, but their relationships felt underexplored. I would have appreciated more insight into their dynamics and interactions.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing was engaging, but the pacing issues and rushed ending detracted from the overall experience.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The world-building was impressive, with a richly detailed setting that added depth to the story.

**Themes:**
The story's themes of deception, betrayal, and redemption were compelling, but the rushed ending left me wanting more exploration of these elements.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story had moments of emotional depth, but the rushed ending diminished the overall impact.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed the story and was eager to continue reading. However, the pacing issues and rushed ending left me feeling unsatisfied.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
I was excited to read this book, and it met many of my expectations. However, the pacing and ending fell short of my hopes.

### Overall Assessment:
Overall, I enjoyed the story and found the world-building and characters to be strong points. However, the pacing issues and rushed ending left me wanting more. Despite this, I would still recommend the book to others, as it has the potential to be a great read with a bit more polish.

### Score: 3.5
