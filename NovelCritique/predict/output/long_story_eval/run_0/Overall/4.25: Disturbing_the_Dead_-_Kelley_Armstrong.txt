### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story was engaging and well-paced, with a plot that kept me hooked from start to finish.

**Characters:**
The characters were well-developed and engaging, with Mallory's modern perspective adding a unique and refreshing twist to the Victorian setting.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing style was engaging and easy to follow, making the story enjoyable to read.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The Victorian setting was vividly portrayed, and the time-travel element added a unique and intriguing layer to the story.

**Themes:**
The story effectively explores themes of time travel, Victorian society, and the challenges of adapting to a different era.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story elicited a range of emotions, from excitement to sadness, and kept me invested throughout.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed this book and found it to be a delightful read.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
I was pleasantly surprised by how much I enjoyed this book, as I had initially been skeptical about the time-travel element.

### Overall Assessment:
This book was a delightful surprise, offering a unique blend of time travel, Victorian society, and modern perspectives. The engaging plot, well-developed characters, and vivid setting made it a thoroughly enjoyable read.

### Score: 4.5
