### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Sadie Wen, a high-achieving student who navigates her life through emails she never intends to send. When these emails are accidentally sent, her carefully crafted image is shattered, and she must confront the consequences. The plot is engaging, and the unexpected twist of the emails adds a unique layer to the narrative.

**Characters:**
Sadie Wen is a well-developed protagonist, and her journey of self-discovery and growth is compelling. The supporting characters, including Julius, Abigail, and Max, enrich the story with their own struggles and relationships. The romance between Sadie and Julius is sweet and well-executed, adding depth to the narrative.

**Writing and Language:**
The writing is engaging and easy to follow, making the story enjoyable from start to finish.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The story is set in a high school, providing a relatable and immersive backdrop for the characters' struggles and relationships.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of self-discovery, relationships, and the consequences of one's actions. It offers a realistic portrayal of high school life, highlighting the challenges and complexities of growing up.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of Sadie's struggles and her journey of self-discovery. The romance between Sadie and Julius adds a heartwarming touch to the narrative.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The story is engaging and easy to follow, making it a quick and enjoyable read.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The novel is a delightful blend of romance, humor, and self-discovery, making it a perfect read for fans of light-hearted and relatable stories.

### Overall Assessment:
This novel is a delightful and engaging read, offering a unique blend of romance, humor, and self-discovery. With well-developed characters, a relatable setting, and a heartwarming romance, it's a story that will resonate with readers of all ages.

### Score: 4.0
