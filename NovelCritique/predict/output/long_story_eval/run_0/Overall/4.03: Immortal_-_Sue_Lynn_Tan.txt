### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Liyen, the heiress to Tianxia, as she navigates her kingdom's obligation to the immortals and her growing attraction to the God of War. The plot is engaging, with a well-paced narrative that keeps readers invested in Liyen's journey.

**Characters:**
Liyen is a compelling protagonist, and her relationship with the God of War is both intense and emotionally resonant. The supporting characters, including Liyen's grandfather and the immortal queen, add depth to the narrative.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing is engaging and immersive, making it easy to become fully invested in the story.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The Immortal Realm is richly detailed, with a unique magic system that enhances the story's fantasy elements.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of love, power, and identity, all of which are central to Liyen's journey.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of Liyen's relationship with the God of War and her struggles with her kingdom's obligations.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
I thoroughly enjoyed this novel and found it to be a captivating read.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
This novel is a perfect blend of fantasy and romance, making it an ideal choice for readers who enjoy both genres.

### Overall Assessment:
This novel is a captivating blend of fantasy and romance, with a well-developed plot, compelling characters, and rich world-building. The emotional depth and immersive writing make it a highly enjoyable read.

### Score: 4.5
