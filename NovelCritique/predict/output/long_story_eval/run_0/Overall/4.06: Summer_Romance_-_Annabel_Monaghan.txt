### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story follows Ali, a professional organizer who has lost her way after her mother's death and her husband's departure. Her life takes a turn when she meets Ethan, a friend from her past, and their connection sparks a romance. The narrative is engaging, and the romance is well-developed, making for a delightful read.

**Characters:**
Ali's journey of self-discovery and healing is beautifully portrayed, making her a relatable and endearing protagonist. Ethan's character adds depth to the story, and their chemistry is palpable.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing is both engaging and heartfelt, making the story feel authentic and emotionally resonant.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The small-town setting of Beechwood, NY, adds charm and warmth to the story, enhancing the overall atmosphere.

**Themes:**
The story explores themes of grief, healing, and the importance of community, which are handled with sensitivity and depth.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story is emotionally resonant, particularly in its portrayal of grief and healing. The author's writing evokes a strong sense of empathy and connection.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
This book is a delightful summer read, perfect for those seeking a light yet emotionally engaging romance.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
This book is ideal for fans of contemporary romance, particularly those who appreciate stories about healing, community, and small-town settings.

### Overall Assessment:
This book is a heartfelt and engaging contemporary romance that explores themes of grief, healing, and community. With its relatable protagonist, charming small-town setting, and well-developed romance, it's a perfect summer read for fans of the genre.

### Score: 4.5
