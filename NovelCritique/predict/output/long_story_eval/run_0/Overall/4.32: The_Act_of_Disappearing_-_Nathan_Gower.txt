### Review:
**Plot and Structure:**
The story's alternating timelines and dual perspectives were executed seamlessly, enhancing the overall narrative.

**Characters:**
Julia's journey of self-discovery and her connection to her grandmother's past were compelling and well-developed.

**Writing and Language:**
The author's writing style is engaging and accessible, making the story easy to follow and enjoy.

**World-Building and Setting:**
The historical setting of 1960s Kentucky was vividly portrayed, enriching the narrative with depth and authenticity.

**Themes:**
The novel explores themes of resilience, survival, and the cyclical nature of history, particularly through the lens of women's struggles.

**Emotional Impact:**
The story evoked strong emotions, particularly in its portrayal of Julia's struggles and her grandmother's sacrifices.

**Enjoyment and Engagement:**
The novel was a quick and enjoyable read, with a narrative that kept me engaged from start to finish.

**Expectation Fulfillment:**
The book's premise was intriguing, and the author's ability to weave a compelling mystery around it was impressive.

### Overall Assessment:
The Act of Disappearing is a captivating and emotionally resonant novel that masterfully blends historical fiction, mystery, and family drama. With its well-crafted plot, vivid setting, and strong character development, it's a story that will leave readers moved and inspired.

### Score: 4.5
